In a world where the fashion industry’s foundation is dishonesty, we believe by upholding honest business morals rooted in the idea of transparency, we can provide future generations with a chance to hold companies accountable as to what they buy.

Founded in 2022 our team is proud to present WORN clothing. We want to break taboos and make the first step to help and/or even solve this problem. In our brand, we care about transparency and honesty next to eco-friendliness. This way our customers know that by wearing our sweaters, they also assist to make the world a better place.

Really, we are all about bringing more hoensty and transparency to the fashion world, and so making it a better place. Meet our team!
