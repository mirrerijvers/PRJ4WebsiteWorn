Brand vision

In a world where the fashion industry’s foundation is dishonesty, we believe by upholding honest business morals rooted in the idea of transparency, we can provide future generations with a chance to hold companies accountable as to what they buy.  

Brand values

Our message is to highlight the purchasing power customers have on the environment. We want to encourage buyers to consider questioning the production during their purchasing decisions. Our hope is customers will begin to evaluate other companies and whether or not they care about larger issues regarding our earth.
Our overall values are…

  • Transparancy
  • Honesty
  • Believing customers have the right to understand every part of our business

    Brand name

    We choose WORN. This name refers to worn clothes, because our hoodies are made of 70% recycled fabric. The name explains the brand and is easy to pronounce.  

    Brand identity

    We want to break taboos and make the first step to help and/or even solve this problem. In our brand, we care about transparency and honesty next to eco-friendliness. This way our customers know that by wearing our sweaters, they also assist to make the world a better place. 

    Social media

    Instagram
    We will utilize instagram to Introduce new products as users scroll through their phones. We will create eye-catching ads with bright colours and diverse models to showcase our sweaters. To further engagement, we plan to reach out with questions asked about our values and catchy quotes. Every piece of our marketing strategy will incorporate the idea of transparency, diversity and overall sustainability. Besides our regular post we want to use Instagram stories to highlight our values and get more interaction with our followers. We choose Instagram as our main focus because our personal interviews showed us that a big part of our target audience uses Instagram on a daily basis. Therefore we think instagram is a good platform to promote and display our products to our possible target audience. We hope that by using Instagram we can raise interest among the users who view our content and create more awareness about the fashion industry problems.

    Tiktok
    The second platform we will use is TikTok. TikTok is growing fast among brands. On this short video platform we plan on making catchy videos where we explain our brand, let them see the hoodies and create more awareness about the fashion industry's problems. The videos we post on TikTok, we want to share them also through Instagram stories. In that way we combine the two platforms. We choose TikTok, because it is the newest and fastest growing social media platform and therefore it is easy to go viral on. Everyone uses it right now. We expect a big part of our target audience to visit TikTok on a daily basis. Therefore we think Tiktok is a good platform to promote and display our promotion to our possible target audience. We hope that by using TikTok we can raise interest among the users who view our content.
